Debug and Release executables are provided in their respectively named folders.



For the program to run, a working camera must be connected to the computer.

While the camera window is in focus:

- Click to up to two points of your preference to select colours for motion tracking.

- With one colour selected you can track rightward, leftward, downward and upward swiping by pressing keys 1 to 4 respectively.

- With an additional colour selected, keys 5, 6 and 7 enable tracking of joint down (clench), joint up (unclench) and scurrying respectively.

- Press R to trigger drawing of the selected colour trajectories.
